Our principal, Thomas S. Love, P.E., has extensive experience in Southern California that provides a comprehensive awareness of the issues and concerns specific to each community. He has acted as Principal in Charge for a variety of projects for both the private and public sector ranging from master planned communities, residential tracts, multi-family housing, retail commercial sites, medical office buildings, and industrial building complexes to various public improvements and special district engineering activities.

Mr. Love maintains an extensive network of contacts within the local public agencies to assure rapid processing of each project. He is known for his creativity in resolving complex issues to the satisfaction of all stakeholders and is highly recruited for his in-depth technical knowledge. Mr. Love remains actively involved in all projects to provide clients with a technically accurate and value engineered deliverable and will be your main point of contact.
